1. Go to the main.py file
2. Click on the Play button OR go to the 'HollowChamber' directory, and run: python3 main.py
3. When in game...
    - Choose your operator.
    - Click on the dice button to roll the dice.
    - Click on the action you want to do. You can either shoot yourself (which will skip the opponent's turn, allowing you to roll the dice 
      again), or shoot the opponent which will give the turn over to them.
    - When on the opponent's turn, click on the big red button which will complete their action.
    - Repeat this until you win, or lose.
    - When on either endscreens, click the play-again button.
    - To exit the game, go to the terminal where you ran the game, and press control+c
